# Elevate-Labs-Internship-Task-4

## Objective

The objective of this project was to design an interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing sales performance, profit trends, and business growth across different regions, categories, and time periods.

## Dataset Used

* Comprehensive Sales Transactions Dataset

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* Microsoft Excel

## Work Performed

* Imported and transformed the sales dataset in Power BI
* Created interactive dashboards with slicers and filters for Year, State, City, and Category
* Designed KPI cards for:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Profit
  * Profit Margin %
  * Total Customers
  * YoY Growth %
* Performed time-series analysis to track yearly growth trends
* Used DAX measures for Sales, Profit, and YoY Growth calculations
* Built category-wise, regional, and city-level performance visualizations
* Applied a professional and consistent dashboard layout for better storytelling

## Key Insights

* Total Sales reached 6M with 2M total profit
* Sales increased by 23.5% YoY, indicating strong business growth
* Office Supplies contributed the highest sales share
* New York and Florida were top-performing states
* Orlando and San Francisco generated strong performance among cities
* High profit margins (~26%) reflected efficient cost management
* Houston and Tampa showed comparatively lower performance
